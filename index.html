👨‍💻 About Me

Hey, I'm Timothy Creekmore — a Data/Computer Scientist with a love for all things technical. Whether it’s computers, the intricacies of sports, or the stock market, it really doesn't matter — I find joy in things with a high skill ceiling that I can improve at over time.

This probably all started when I was 12. I played (and still do) the online game World of Tanks on an old Acer laptop my parents gave me. That laptop constantly overheated, so I had to take it apart and clean out the exhaust fan. After that, it worked great. From then on, I’ve always had a love for computers.

As I got older, my dad roped me into becoming an appliance repair technician at 16. I worked for his company — Alpine Appliance — off and on for about six years while juggling school and college. That job helped me develop real technical skills in diagnosing and solving problems. Eventually, I transitioned into the used appliance market — kind of like flipping cars, but smaller. Everyone needs appliances. Now, I’ll occasionally find broken ones, fix them, and resell them — using the same skills I started developing at 12 and sharpened on the job.

Today, I’m fascinated by data and machine learning. Being able to harness insights from data is like having a superpower — and with how fast data is growing, there’s never been a better time to dive in. I’m especially passionate about projects that give people the ability to make better-informed decisions.

When I’m not working on a portfolio project, you’ll most likely find me playing strategy games, playing sports, or fixing a washer under my porch.

I’m married to my beautiful wife, Elise, and one of our big goals is to start a homestead on a nice piece of land — and somehow raise 8 kids. But for now, we’re just taking it one day at a time.
